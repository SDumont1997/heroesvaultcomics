All about comics

What is a comic?

The comic is a series of drawp that constitute a story, with or without text, based on Will Eisner's conception of this graphic narrative as a sequential art, juxtaposed illustrations and other sequential images with the purpose of transmitting an aesthetic comic to the reader. From Webcomics.com we provide you with access to the comics posted on the network, at no time do we assume their ownership or authorship.

Comic benefits:

  • - Encourage reading through comics.
  • - Communicate through images.
  • - Stimulate the methods of analysis and synthesis.
  • - Develop communication skills by learning the most used expressions in everyday language.
  • - Encourage creativity and imagination.
  • - Transfer thoughts and ideas to the formal structures provided by written language.
  • - Develop collaborative work.
